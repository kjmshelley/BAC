What is Virtual Company Journey (VCJ)?

A new UNIQUE learning experience

The VCJ approach will help you to learn how to navigate the software industry. Choose from over 10 industries to build a real world SaaS application with one of our expert mentors. You will learn how to start a project from beginning to end. That means, you will learn about requirements, mock ups, ticket systems, coding repository strategies, and of course coding! Throughout this process, you will have to learn how to talk to hiring managers, product managers, and QA testers. These are alls skills that every software developers need.
